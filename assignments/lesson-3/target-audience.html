Target Audience:

The target audience for weatheradventurewarriors.com web site is described below:

  • Who: Hikers, Campers, Bird watchers, Fisherman, Rock Climbers, Rafters/Canoeing and others who just want to take in the beauty of nature. Also for people who work in the parks and for parents to check for school cancellations.
  • For all ages who wish to disconnect from the modern world.
  • Motivation: The knowledge of knowing that you come in contact with nature and being one with yourself. Improve your skills with nature. Also to have a piece of mind knowing that your children are safe and not in danger when traveling to and from school.
